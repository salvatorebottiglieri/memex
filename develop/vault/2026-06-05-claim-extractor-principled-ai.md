# ClaimExtractor-2605 - SLMs for claim and intent extraction

Principled Intelligence released **ClaimExtractor** on Hugging Face: open-weight small language models (SLMs) for fast/accurate claim and intent extraction from any message in a conversation. Two model sizes: claim-extractor-2B-q-2605 and claim-extractor-4B-q-2605.

**Problem:** Most conversational AI stacks treat messages as unstructured text, but every message carries implicit claims (what the user/assistant is asserting) and intents (what they're trying to do).

**Use cases:** Intent routing, guardrailing, clustering, monitoring, fact-checking, claim auditing, and human-in-the-loop supervision.

Part of a continued effort to democratize AI oversight for responsible AI deployment.
